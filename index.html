About Us

MS Interiors is a professionally managed interior design and execution firm that boasts a team of highly skilled and experienced interior designers. With expertise in both residential and commercial spaces, MS Interiors excels in delivering exceptional designs that transform any space into a work of art.

MS Interiors has a wealth of experience spanning over 15 years. The firm specializes in designing residential, commercial, and retail spaces across Bangalore, one of the world’s top IT hubs.

Our team is dedicated to crafting interiors that reflect our client's unique style and preferences. From sophisticated false ceilings and elegant kitchens to expansive dining areas, custom wardrobes, luxurious living rooms, and modern office designs – MS Interiors covers every aspect of interior design. We also specialize in creating beautiful bathroom interiors, playful kids' rooms, and state-of-the-art gym spaces.
